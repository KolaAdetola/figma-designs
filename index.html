1

Discovery

Have ideas, concepts, business plans or goals?
Tell us! and we will come up with the best
possible software solutions and service to
actualise it. Discuss your requirements with us,
we will visualise them and create a clearly
defined scope for your project. We do this in
such a way to allow and enable flexibility should
your requirements change along the way.

2

Design

With a viable project scope, we put together a
team of skilled and exceptional designers and
software developers who will design and
implement quality solutions. We maintain
communication with you throughout our planning
and execution process to implement all your
requirements into the project architecture.

3

Development

It's time to bring your product to life now that we
have your design. Using agile methodology, Our
team breaks down your product into manageable
and measurable chunks called sprints and works
on them in a time and priority-based order to
deliver measurable and quality results.By utilizing
Ci/CD practices, we will ensure that your
applications are always updated without
downtime.

4

Testing

Our testing process occurs at the end of every
sprint to ensure the software requirements are
met, bugs our system picks are then iterated into
the oncoming sprint. This is to ensure we stick to
development time while maintaining the quality
and integrity of your product.

5

Deployment

Deployment is automated, which means that new
features of your product are readily available.
After passing each testing phase and meeting all
sprint goals, every feature is integrated and
undergoes final testing. After this, your software
is released to the public.

6

Maintenance

Part of our services to you is the maintenance
and management of your software database,
data warehouses and applications. Our team
maintains the functionality of your product by
dealing with bugs, faults and implementing new
features and ideas. Other maintenance services
include software project rescue and software
modernisation.
